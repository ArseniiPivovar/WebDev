God of War (2005)

Story

The goddess Athena tasks Kratos with killing Ares, the God of War and Kratos' former mentor who tricked Kratos into killing his wife and daughter. As Ares besieges Athens out of hatred for Athena, Kratos embarks on a quest to find the one object capable of stopping the god once and for all: Pandora's Box.

The Ghost of Sparta

After Kratos was tricked into killing his wife and daughter, a village oracle cursed Kratos and condemned him to wear the "mark of his terrible deed"; the ashes of his family, which turn his skin pale white, earning him the title "Ghost of Sparta"

More Info

The original God of war was released on March 22, 2005. It was playable only on the Playstation 2 but was remastered on August 28, 2012 for the Playstation 3. God of War is loosly based on Greek mythology.
