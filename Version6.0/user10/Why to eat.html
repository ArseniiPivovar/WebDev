Foods to avoid, 83 Unhealthiest Fast Foods On the Planet(I'm not doing all 83):

    1. Mcdonald's Meal(Fast Food)
It can be addictive because it's quick, tasty and other things. I'm just saying respectfully not to go to mcdonalds there's not a lot of benefits. Most of the items, from breakfast and beyond, are high in calories, fat, and sodium.

    2. Classic Bacon and Cheese Chicken Sandwich(Fast Food)
It's one of the highest-sodium, highest-calorie items on the Popeyes menu. This sandwich has more saturated fat per serving than any other fast food chicken sandwich we could find. /div>
    3. Burger King's Double Sausage, Egg, & Cheese Biscuit(Fast Food)
Look closely there's not one but two, "this small sandwich has two". It also comes on a biscuit, which contributes to the insanely high amount of fat (almost as much as in McDonald's entire Big Breakfast platter) and the crazy level of sodium.
